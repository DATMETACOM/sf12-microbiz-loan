### Shinhan Bank Vietnam

Nhieu use case tap trung vao:

- AI personal financial coach trong `SOL app`
- customer engagement automation
- loyalty va personalized offers
- AI call bot cho collections va sales
- internal reporting va BI bang giao dien hoi dap
- embedded installment va BNPL
- SoftPOS cho micro business
- voice biometrics chong gian lan
- SME credit scoring dua tren `POS data`

Trong do, use case `AI-Driven SME Credit Scoring via POS Data` rat gan voi huong `Shinhan SME Credit Copilot` dang co trong wiki.

- voice-to-text solution

Neu can mo rong y tuong theo huong tai chinh, day la nhom de bai co kha nang demo rat tot.

# Idea 4 - Tasco Service Advisor Copilot

## Van de

Khach hang mo ta loi xe mo ho, co van dich vu phai hoi lai nhieu, lich hen va phu tung chuan bi chua toi uu.

## Giai phap

Copilot cho front-desk va service advisor trong quy trinh tiep nhan xe.

Input:

- voice note cua khach
- anh/video van de tren xe
- lich su bao duong
- thong tin dong xe

Output:

- phan loai muc do khan cap
- nguyen nhan kha di
- danh sach kiem tra cho ky thuat vien
- phu tung nen chuan bi truoc
- de xuat lich hen

## Tai sao de thang

- Multimodal demo rat dep.
- Sat nghiep vu sau ban hang.
- Co the cho thay tac dong van hanh ro rang.

## Rui ro

- Can demo rat gon de tranh thanh "chan doan xe bang AI" qua tham.
- Nen dinh vi la pre-diagnosis support, khong phai ket luan ky thuat cuoi.

## Muc do uu tien

`Trung binh`

# Winning Strategy

## Cong thuc de co xac suat thang cao

1. Chon `mot workflow duy nhat`.
2. Chuan bi `5-10 case demo` that chat.
3. Lam output co the kiem chung duoc.
4. Trinh bay ROI theo ngon ngu doanh nghiep.
5. Chot ro vi sao Qwen phu hop.

## Muc tieu demo

Demo tot nhat la mot chuoi ngan:

`upload input -> AI phan tich -> tom tat van de -> de xuat hanh dong -> tao tai lieu hoac action`

## Cach pitch

- Mo bang pain point trong 20 giay.
- Cho thay quy trinh cu cham va loi.
- Demo 1 case dep va 1 case loi.
- Chot metric tac dong.
- Ket bang kha nang pilot voi doi tac.

## Vi sao idea 1 dang manh nhat

`Shinhan SME Credit Copilot` co cac diem vuot troi:

- Du lieu dau vao da dang: sao ke, hoa don, hop dong, KYC.
- Co quyet dinh nghiep vu ro: de nghi tham dinh, can bo sung gi, can xem xet gi.
- Co kha nang tao artifact kinh doanh: credit memo, missing-doc checklist, risk summary.
- Co the demo bang bo ho so gia lap ma van rat thuyet phuc.

# Official Resources

Trang nay tong hop noi dung cong khai tu muc `Resources` cua Devpost de team co mot diem tham chieu nhanh cho huong build, cong nghe duoc khuyen nghi, va kenh ho tro chinh thuc.

Nguon goc: https://qwen-ai-build-day.devpost.com/resources

## Introduction

Organizer mo ta hackathon nay khong chi de lam mot demo cho dep, ma de day team di tu `experimentation` sang `execution`.

Dieu nay co nghia:

- bai thi nen giai mot use case thuc te
- can the hien tu duy san pham ro hon, khong chi la prototype chung chung
- san pham nen du suc de duoc judge, doi tac he sinh thai, va stakeholder doanh nghiep xem nhu mot huong co the di tiep

## Tools and Technologies

Trang resources hien tai khuyen khich participant build voi:

- `Qwen`
- `Alibaba Cloud`
- cac `approved APIs`, framework, va open-source tools phu hop voi giai phap

Mot ghi chu quan trong la `full technical guidance`, challenge context, va cac resource duoc de xuat se duoc organizer chia se them trong kickoff va qua cac kenh ho tro chinh thuc.

## Inspiration

Huong dan tu organizer rat ro rang:

- bat dau bang mot bai toan that
- giu solution gon va de hieu
- show mot demo thuc su chay duoc

Theo trang resources, bai thi manh thuong lam tot 3 diem:

1. giai ro `mot van de cu the`
2. co `working demo`, khong chi dung o concept
3. lam ro `vi sao giai phap quan trong`

## Implications for This Wiki

Tac dong truc tiep len cach chon idea trong wiki nay:

- uu tien workflow hep, co mot pain point duy nhat
- demo phai co input va output ro rang
- can noi ro gia tri kinh doanh hoac operational impact
- nen xem `Qwen` la thanh phan cot loi cua workflow, khong chi la lop chat
- neu can cloud hoac ha tang demo, `Alibaba Cloud` la lua chon phu hop voi dinh huong organizer

## Recommended Team Checklist

Truoc khi chot huong build, team nen tu check nhanh:

1. Idea co giai quyet `mot van de cu the` khong?
2. Co demo end-to-end trong vai phut khong?
3. Gia tri cua output co de judge hieu ngay khong?
4. Vai tro cua `Qwen` co that su trung tam khong?
5. Neu can support hoac clarification, da theo doi kickoff va kenh organizer chua?

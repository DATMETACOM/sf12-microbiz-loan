# Contest Analysis

## Tom tat brief cong khai

Theo brief cong khai tren Luma va Devpost, cuoc thi co cac dac diem chinh:

- Build window ngan, tap trung vao prototype co the demo nhanh.
- Co nhieu track gan voi doanh nghiep va khu vuc cong.
- Giam khao cham khong chi bang muc do "hay" cua idea, ma bang muc do giai quyet dung pain point va chat luong thuc thi.

## Tieu chi cham diem can toi uu

Nam cum canh tranh can duoc toi uu dong thoi:

1. `Problem relevance`
   Giai bai toan that, gap that, ton tien that.
2. `Solution quality`
   Flow hop ly, output huu ich, dung duoc trong quy trinh.
3. `Use of AI`
   AI tao gia tri cot loi, khong phai lop trang tri.
4. `Use of Qwen`
   Chon workflow ma Qwen co ly do ton tai ro rang.
5. `Execution and demo`
   Demo phai muot, co input mau, co output thuyet phuc.

## Dieu giam khao thuong thich

- Workflow hep nhung khoi dung duoc ngay.
- Co du lieu dau vao that: file, anh, giay to, log, voice, video.
- Co metric kinh doanh de noi: giam thoi gian xu ly, giam loi, tang conversion, tang adherence.
- Co reasoning va action output, khong chi la chat.

## Dieu de rot

- Idea qua rong, muon "lam sieu ung dung AI".
- Chi co giao dien chat, khong co quy trinh nghiep vu.
- Khong co bo sample data de demo.
- Khong noi ro vi sao lai phai dung Qwen.

## Nhan dinh chien luoc

Muon thang thi nen tap trung vao bai toan `document-heavy`, `multimodal`, `long-context`, hoac `decision-support`.

Do la cac bai toan:

- Co input phuc tap.
- Co nhieu buoc suy luan.
- Co the danh gia chat luong output.
- Kho thay bang mot chatbot FAQ.

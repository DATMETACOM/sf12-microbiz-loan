Eligibility
Qwen AI Build Day is open to developers, startups, builders, students, and technical teams who want to build practical AI solutions.

Participants may join individually or as a team, subject to the final team-size rules set by the organizers.

 

All participants must register through the official event channels and comply with Devpost’s terms, the event rules, and the organizer’s instructions.

 

Event Day Attendance Requirement
All teams are required to attend Qwen AI Build Day on 21 April 2026 in person, or send at least one official team representative.

Teams that are absent on event day and do not have a representative present will be disqualified.

 

On the event day, the Top 10 shortlisted teams for each track will be announced. Only shortlisted teams will earn the opportunity to pitch live to the judging panel.

 
Project and Submission Requirements
To be eligible:

 

the project must be submitted on time through Devpost
the project must align with an official challenge track
the submission must include the required materials
the team must clearly explain how Qwen and/or Alibaba Cloud technologies were used
the project must be original, or clearly disclose any pre-existing work, open-source components, or third-party tools used
the team must attend the event day, or send at least one representative
 
Prizes
There will be 1 Track Winner for each official track. Track Winners — USD 1,000 each

1. Track Winner — Mobility Track by Tasco — USD 1,000

In addition, the winning team(s) for the Open Mobility Challenge will receive USD 10,000 seed investment (awarded to up to 3 high-potential teams). The winning team for the Learning & Development Challenge will receive a USD 2,000 POC contract.

 

2. Track Winner — Financial Services Track by Shinhan Future's Lab — USD 1,000

In addition, the winning team will receive priority consideration for up to VND 200,000,000 in PoC funding per startup, awarded for successful PoC outcomes through Global Shinhan InnoBoost 2026.

 

3. Track Winner — Healthcare Track by Elfie — USD 1,000

In addition, the winning team will receive consideration for a poc contract of up to VND 1,000,000,000 (scope-dependent), plus 3+ job contract offers for standout builders.

 

4. Track Winner — Public Sector Track (Government) — USD 1,000

 

Additional partner awards, ecosystem opportunities, or showcase opportunities may be announced by the organizers if applicable.

 

Judging Criteria and Winner Selection
Projects will be evaluated based on:

problem relevance — does the project solve a real and meaningful problem?
quality of solution — is the product clear, usable, and logically designed?
use of AI — is AI meaningfully integrated into the solution?
use of Qwen / Alibaba Cloud — does the team effectively use the supported technologies?
execution — is there a working prototype or convincing demo?
pitch clarity — can the team clearly explain the product, the user, and the value?
Up to 10 shortlisted teams for each track will be invited to pitch live on the event day in front of judges consisting of enterprise representatives and industry experts.

From the shortlisted teams, the judges will select 1 Track Winner for each official track.

Organizer and judge decisions are final.

About the challenge
Qwen AI Build Day is where builders turn ideas into working AI products.

We’re bringing together developers, startups, and AI builders to solve real enterprise-backed challenges using Qwen and Alibaba Cloud technologies.

Confirmed tracks:

Mobility Track by Tasco
Financial Services Track by Shinhan Future's Lab
Healthcare Track by Elfie
Public Sector (Government)
This is not a “build something random and hope for the best” hackathon.

You’ll get:

real problem statements from enterprise-backed tracks
a defined build window
the chance to be shortlisted among (up to)Top 10 teams for your track
the opportunity to pitch live in front of enterprise representatives and industry experts
the chance to become a Track Winner
the chance to present at ALIBABA CLOUD SME AI GROWTH DAY VIETNAM 2026 on 22 April 2026
Whether you’re an engineer, technical founder, product builder, or student with strong execution skills, this is your chance to build something useful, prove what you can do, and get in front of the right people.

 
Get started
Here’s how the program works:

Step 1 — Register
Sign up on Devpost and review the challenge details.

Step 2 — Join the kickoff webinar
Attend the kickoff webinar on 10 April 2026 to understand the challenge tracks, expectations, and how to build with Qwen / Alibaba Cloud.

Step 3 — Choose your track
Build for one of the official challenge tracks:

Mobility Track by Tasco
Financial Services Track by Shinhan Future's Lab
Healthcare Track by Elfie
Public Sector (Government)
Step 4 — Build your solution
Teams will build during the official build period from 11–17 April 2026.

Step 5 — Submit on Devpost
Complete your project submission before the deadline with your demo, write-up, and required links.

Step 6 — Attend Qwen AI Build Day
All teams must attend the live event on 21 April 2026 in Ho Chi Minh City, or send at least one team representative. Teams that do not show up will be disqualified.

Step 7 — Pitch if shortlisted
On the event day, the Top 10 shortlisted teams for each track will be announced. Shortlisted teams will earn the opportunity to pitch live in front of judges consisting of enterprise representatives and industry experts.

Step 8 — Win your track

Each official track will have 1 Track Winner, awarded USD 1,000. Track Winners are selected by the judges from the shortlisted teams who pitched live on event day.

Step 9 — Advance to the final showcase
The track winners will present at ALIBABA CLOUD SME AI GROWTH DAY VIETNAM 2026 on 22 April 2026. 

Requirements
What to Build
Build a practical AI solution using Qwen and/or Alibaba Cloud technologies.

Your project should:

solve a real user or business problem
clearly show how AI is used in the product
demonstrate a working prototype, MVP, or functional demo
align with one of the official challenge tracks shared by the organizers
be built during the official hackathon period, with clear attribution for any pre-existing components
Confirmed official tracks:

Mobility Track by Tasco (View Use Cases)
Financial Services Track by Shinhan Future's Lab (View Use Cases)
Healthcare Track by Elfie (View Use Cases)
Public Sector Track (Government) (View Use Cases)
 

We’re looking for projects that are useful, clear, and well executed — not just ambitious ideas.

 
What to Submit
Each team must submit the following on Devpost:

project name
team member details
short project summary
problem statement
explanation of how the solution works
which Qwen and/or Alibaba Cloud tools were used
demo video
product screenshots
live demo link or repository link, if available
pitch deck or supporting materials, if available
 

Make your submission easy to understand. A strong idea with a weak submission will lose to a clear project that judges can evaluate quickly.

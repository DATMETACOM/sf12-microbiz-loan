# Idea 2 - Elfie Adherence Rescue Agent

## Van de

Benh nhan man tinh thuong bo thuoc, bo do chi so, va mat dong luc sau vai tuan su dung app.

## Giai phap

Mot AI agent phat hien nguy co non-adherence som va can thiep dung luc.

Dau vao:

- lich su nhac nho
- nhat ky do chi so
- tan suat bo qua thuoc
- dau hieu giam tuong tac

Dau ra:

- `risk score` cho tung benh nhan
- thong diep nhac nho ca nhan hoa
- tom tat cho health coach
- de xuat escalate sang nguoi cham soc neu can

## Tai sao de thang

- Problem rat ro va co tinh xa hoi.
- Co metric kha de noi: adherence, retention, rescue rate.
- De demo bang timeline gia lap cua mot benh nhan.

## Ranh gioi an toan

Khong dinh vi la cong cu chan doan.
Chi dinh vi la `engagement and adherence copilot`.

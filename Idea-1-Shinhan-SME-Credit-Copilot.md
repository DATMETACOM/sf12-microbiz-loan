# Idea 1 - Shinhan SME Credit Copilot

## Van de

Ho so SME vay von thuong phan tan, thieu thong tin, va ton nhieu cong suc cua RM va credit analyst de:

- doc sao ke
- doi chieu hoa don va hop dong
- kiem tra thieu giay to
- viet credit memo
- tong hop red flags

## Giai phap

Mot copilot noi bo cho quy trinh pre-screening va memo drafting.

He thong nhan:

- bank statements
- invoices
- tax filings
- KYC docs
- contracts

Sau do tra ra:

- `missing document checklist`
- `cashflow summary`
- `business health snapshot`
- `risk flags`
- `draft credit memo`
- `recommended next step`

## Tai sao de thang

- Dung bai toan co gia tri kinh doanh cao.
- Demo rat de hieu voi giam khao.
- Qwen duoc dung vao dau vao da dinh dang va bo ngu canh dai.
- Co output cu the, khong mo ho.

## Demo flow goi y

1. Upload bo ho so SME mau.
2. He thong tu dong trich xuat va gom thong tin.
3. He thong chi ra 3 red flags va 2 tai lieu con thieu.
4. He thong tao draft credit memo 1 trang.
5. Nhan vien bam `Review` de chinh lai va export.

## KPI pitch

- Giam 60-80 phan tram thoi gian pre-screening.
- Giam bo sot giay to.
- Tang toc do phan hoi khach hang SME.

## Muc do uu tien

`Rất cao`

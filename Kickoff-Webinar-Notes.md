# Kickoff Webinar Notes

Trang nay tong hop nhung diem quan trong tu file transcript `qwenday.txt` cua buoi kickoff webinar `Qwen AI Build Day`.

## Core Positioning

Theo organizer, day khong phai mot hackathon thong thuong. Day la mot `enterprise-backed AI build day`, nghia la:

- challenge statement den tu nhu cau that cua doanh nghiep
- bai du thi co the mo ra co hoi `POC` voi enterprise
- neu di dung huong, giai phap co the co duong di tiep thanh san pham that

## Build Window and Timeline

Nhung moc thoi gian duoc nhac ro trong webinar:

- build period: tu sau kickoff den het `17/04`, `11:59 PM` gio Viet Nam
- remote evaluation: sau khi het han nop bai
- shortlist pitch day: `21/04`
- winner showcase tai `Alibaba Cloud SME AI Growth Day`: `22/04`

Mot diem quan trong: neu duoc shortlist, team can co `it nhat 1 dai dien` den pitch truc tiep vao ngay `21/04`.

## Submission Mechanics

Organizer huong dan rat ky tren Devpost:

- nen tao project va bam `Submit` som, ke ca khi moi la ban nhap muc cao
- team, track, va noi dung submission co the sua truoc deadline
- neu khong bam `Submit`, bai co the van o trang thai `draft` va bi xem la chua nop
- co the nop `nhieu project`, nhung moi project phai la mot submission rieng
- mot project co the chon `nhieu track` neu giai quyet duoc nhieu challenge statement

## Judging Criteria

Webinar nhac lai 4 tru cot danh gia:

1. `Problem relevance`
2. `Solution quality`
3. `Use of AI`
4. `Execution and demo`

Mot yeu cau xuyen suot la bai thi `phai dung Qwen`.

## What Organizers Want to See

Theo phan huong dan submission va evaluation:

- mo ta project du thong tin tren Devpost vi vong dau cham tu xa
- co `GitHub`, `demo link`, va `video walkthrough`
- giai thich ro vi sao dung `Qwen`
- demo phai giup judge hieu ro user journey va gia tri output

## Team Formation Notes

- toi da `4 nguoi` trong mot team
- co the lap team truoc deadline, khong bat buoc xong ngay kickoff
- mot ca nhan co the tham gia hon mot team
- co the them hoac sua thanh vien truoc han nop bai

## Resource and Support Notes

Organizer cho biet:

- learning resources va cloud-credit guidance da duoc dua len Devpost
- se tiep tuc cap nhat them tai lieu sau kickoff
- participant nen theo doi `WhatsApp support group` va email update
- ngoai Devpost, team cung duoc nhac dang ky `Luma` de phuc vu attendance/check-in
